Ada Lovelace

Mathematician

Augusta Ada King-Noel, Countess of Lovelace was an English mathematician and writer, chiefly known for her work on Charles Babbage's proposed mechanical general-purpose computer, the Analytical Engine. Wikipedia

  • Born: 10 December 1815, London
  • Died: 27 November 1852, Marylebone
  • Full name: Augusta Ada King
  • Spouse: William King-Noel, 1st Earl of Lovelace (m. 1835–1852)
  • Children: Anne Blunt, 15th Baroness Wentworth, more
  • Parents: George Gordon Byron, Anne Isabella Byron, Baroness Byron
